Biography

Mehmet Akif Ersoy (1873–1936) was a Turkish poet, writer, academic, and politician. He is best known as the author of the Turkish National Anthem, "İstiklal Marşı." Throughout his life, Ersoy advocated for Islamic values and unity, while producing works that addressed the social and political struggles of his time.

His most renowned poetry collection, "Safahat," reflects his deep connection to his faith and his love for his nation. Mehmet Akif was also a teacher, a veterinarian, and a journalist. His vision and efforts significantly influenced Turkey's national identity during its most challenging years.
